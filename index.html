Woven by Heart, Whispered Through Verses

'Tis the art of expression radiating within a timeless harmony. Poetry, a language of soul, whose words entice elegance, breathes symphonies of one's heart 

                        'Tis icy wind
                        sweeping life with bated breath
                        as snow amassed its soul
                        footsteps heard in the distance
                        pwning silence, maiden noise
                        degombling
                    
                        Mother's skeptical
                            of the world
                        turned away, babbling things
                            for I believed
                        linked to my nerdy head
                            still in disbelief,
                        asking her own kid to
                            get his head checked
                        by the lecture of university
                            I swear
                        I saw heaven
                            heaven-like world,
                        none of this world
                            could ever imagine
                        mother panting, running,
                            sweating as if
                        caught red-handed
                            her pupils enlarged,
                        her body almost stumbled
                            outside of the house,
                        I witnessed heaven
                            came down it went,
                        beautifully painted,
                            irresistibly desired
                        heaven came for me
                            God has granted,
                        hence in submission
                            I smiled for the gift
                        amidst the flock of people
                    
                    Call me messenger/unlike Moses who
                    spread truth/I spread
                    delicacy and adversity
                    some radiant there/some clouds
                    disguise some rains
                    dawn calling the duty/
                    yellow air is such/
                    a hospitality
                    mind not the city/for there is
                    nothing to worry/along the city centre/
                    are black looking bags/filled with
                    no-one-knows-what-it-is materials
                    here come the crows/
                    such a fine sight to see/
                    permanent residents of the city/
                    nothing foul/nothing peculiar/
                    perhaps some odor/of cadaverine and/
                    putrescine
                    halt the startle/it's the scent of life/
                    behave yourself/I beg you
                    approaching the exit/lifeless form/
                    guarding in a heavy-looking armor/
                    nonetheless/holding their so-called/
                    pointy iron/covered in red liquid/
                    dripping elegantly
                    such a poetry in motion/off I go
